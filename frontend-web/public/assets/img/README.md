# Image Assets Directory

Place your business images in this folder. The website expects the following files:

## Required Images

### Hero & Background
- `hero-bg.jpg` - Main banner background (recommended: 1920x1080 or larger)
- `about-hero-bg.jpg` - About page hero background

### Service Photos (800x600 or larger recommended)
- `service-1.jpg` - Split Klima / Air Conditioner photo
- `service-2.jpg` - VRF Systems photo  
- `service-3.jpg` - Kombi / Boiler photo
- `service-4.jpg` - Installation / Montaj photo

### About Us Section
- `about-us-1.jpg` - Technician or team at work photo
- `about-us-2.jpg` - Office or workspace photo

### Team Member Photos (400x400 or square recommended)
- `team-1.jpg` - Team member 1 photo
- `team-2.jpg` - Team member 2 photo
- `team-3.jpg` - Team member 3 photo
- `team-4.jpg` - Team member 4 photo

## Image Tips

1. **Optimize images** - Use tools like TinyPNG or Squoosh to compress images
2. **Use JPG format** for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
3. **Recommended dimensions**:
   - Hero images: 1920x1080 or larger
   - Service cards: 800x600 minimum
   - Team photos: 400x400 (square)
4. **Keep file sizes under 500KB** for faster loading

## Placeholder Images

If you don't have images yet, you can use placeholder services like:
- https://unsplash.com (free stock photos)
- https://placeholder.com (placeholder images)
